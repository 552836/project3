Welcome to Wild Rescues, a nonprofit wildlife rescue and rehabilitation organization located in Ocala, Florida.

We provide care for injured, sick, neglected, and orphaned wildlife and work hard to rehabilitate and release them back into their natural habitats whenever possible.

Your support helps us rescue more animals, provide medical treatment, and educate the community about protecting local wildlife.
